Crispy Homemade French Fries

Crispy homemade French fries are a timeless and irresistible snack that can be enjoyed as a side dish or a satisfying treat on their own. This recipe transforms humble potatoes into golden, perfectly crispy fries that are seasoned to perfection. Whether you're serving them as a game day snack, a movie night favorite, or a side to your favorite burger, these fries are sure to delight your taste buds with their crispy exterior and fluffy interior.

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes, peeled and cut into even matchsticks
  • 4 cups vegetable oil, for frying
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Ketchup or your favorite dipping sauces

Instructions

  1. Start by peeling the potatoes and cutting them into uniform matchstick-shaped pieces. Rinse them under cold water to remove excess starch.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large, deep pot to 325째F (163째C). A deep fryer can also be used for this step.
  3. In batches, carefully add the potato matchsticks to the hot oil. Fry them for about 3-4 minutes or until they are just beginning to turn golden. Use a slotted spoon to remove them from the oil and place them on paper towels to drain.
  4. Raise the oil temperature to 375째F (190째C).
  5. In a bowl, mix the salt, garlic powder, paprika, black pepper, onion powder, and cayenne pepper. Toss the partially cooked fries in the seasoning mixture to coat them evenly.
  6. Once the oil is at the right temperature, return the seasoned fries to the oil in batches. Fry them for an additional 3-4 minutes or until they are crispy and golden brown.
  7. Remove the fries from the oil and drain them on fresh paper towels. Season them with a pinch of salt while they are still hot.
  8. Transfer the crispy homemade French fries to a serving dish, garnish with chopped fresh parsley, and serve them with your favorite dipping sauces, such as ketchup, mayonnaise, or aioli.

These homemade French fries are the epitome of comfort food. With their perfect crispiness and a delightful blend of seasonings, they're ideal for sharing with friends and family, or simply enjoying on your own as a delectable snack.
