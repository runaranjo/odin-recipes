Lasagna


Description

Classic homemade lasagna is a beloved Italian comfort dish that's perfect for family gatherings and special occasions. Layered with rich and savory flavors, this lasagna recipe combines tender sheets of pasta, a flavorful meat sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, and layers of melted cheese. The result is a hearty, satisfying dish that's sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef or ground Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked al dente
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until they become translucent.
  2. Add the ground beef or Italian sausage to the skillet. Cook until browned and crumbled, breaking it into small pieces as it cooks.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese and grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce. Place three lasagna noodles on top.
  7. Add a layer of the ricotta and Parmesan cheese mixture, followed by a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layering process, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and mozzarella cheese on top.
  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  11. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley.
  12. Slice and serve your classic homemade lasagna, and enjoy the layers of flavor and comfort.
