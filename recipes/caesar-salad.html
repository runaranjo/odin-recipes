Classic Caesar Salad

Description

The classic Caesar salad is a time-honored favorite known for its bold flavors and satisfying combination of crisp romaine lettuce, creamy Caesar dressing, crunchy croutons, and savory Parmesan cheese. This recipe offers a simple yet elegant dish that's perfect as an appetizer, side salad, or a refreshing main course. Whether you're hosting a dinner party or enjoying a quiet meal, the Caesar salad is a versatile and timeless choice that never goes out of style.

Ingredients

  1. For the Salad:

    • 1 head of fresh romaine lettuce, torn into bite-sized pieces
    • 1 cup croutons (homemade or store-bought)
    • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
    • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

  2. For the Caesar Dressing:

    • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
    • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
    • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
    • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. For the Caesar Dressing:
    1. In a bowl, combine the mayonnaise, lemon juice, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and grated Parmesan cheese. Mix until the dressing is smooth and well combined.
    2. Season the dressing with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning as needed. You can add more lemon juice or Worcestershire sauce for extra tanginess if desired.
  2. For the Salad:
    1. In a large salad bowl, add the torn romaine lettuce leaves.
    2. Drizzle the prepared Caesar dressing over the lettuce. Toss gently to coat the lettuce leaves evenly with the dressing.
    3. Sprinkle the salad with the croutons and freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
    4. Finish with a pinch of freshly ground black pepper to enhance the flavor.
    5. Serve your classic Caesar salad as a delightful appetizer, side dish, or main course. Optionally, you can add grilled chicken, shrimp, or anchovies for a protein boost.

The classic Caesar salad is a versatile and timeless favorite that's perfect for various occasions. Its delightful combination of crisp greens, creamy dressing, and savory toppings makes it a well-loved choice for salad enthusiasts everywhere.
